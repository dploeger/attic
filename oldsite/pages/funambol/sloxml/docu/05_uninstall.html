5. Uninstalling SloxML

Introduction

In case you don't like SloxML, it doesn't work for you (please read Troubleshooting first!) or you don't need it anymore, you can safely uninstall SloxML.

Uninstalling the module

  • Go to <funambol-home>/ds-server and open “install.properties” in your favourite text-editor
  • Move the entry for sloxml from the install-modules-line to the uninstall-modules line.
  • Save the file
  • Run bin/install-modules(.cmd or .sh depending on your OS) tomcat50
  • After that the module is uninstalled from the funambol-server
  • Go to modules and remove the sloxml-s4j-module.

Removing the JNDI name

  • Go to <funambol-home>/tools/tomcat/conf/Catalina/localhost and open “funambol.txt” in your favourite text editor.
  • Remove the complete “ResourceName” and “ResourceParams”-tags and subtags referring to the used JNDI-name (i.e.: sloxmlds)
  • Save the file
  • Restart tomcat by restarting the funambol-server

Remove the extra classes

  • Remove the postgresql.jar and the ldap.jar from <funambol-Home>/ds-server/lib/ and <funambol-home>/tools/tomcat/common/lib/.

That's all to remove the sloxml-module and required additional setups. The removal of the funambol-server is not covered by me. Under windows, it should be a simple deinstallation, under Unix-type OS you can remove the complete funambol-home and remove all init-scripts, that would start the server. But, again, the detail removal instructions are not covered here.
